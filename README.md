## MyCryptoCheckout Introduction

MyCryptoCheckout is a cryptocurrency payment gateway plugin designed specifically for WordPress, WooCommerce, and Easy Digital Downloads. Launched in January 2018, it pioneered the peer-to-peer approach to crypto payments by becoming the first gateway to support Ethereum and ERC20 tokens without intermediaries.

What makes MyCryptoCheckout stand out is its commitment to true decentralization. Unlike traditional payment processors, there's no middleman between you and your customers. Payments go directly from the customer's wallet to your wallet with absolutely zero transaction fees. The plugin supports over 100 cryptocurrencies including Bitcoin, Ethereum, Binance Coin, Solana, Litecoin, Dogecoin, and numerous ERC-20, BEP-20, and TRC-20 tokens.
